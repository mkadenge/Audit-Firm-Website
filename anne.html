Anne Wagaki

Anne Wagaki is the Chief Operating Officer at Ronalds LLP. She holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematical Sciences with IT majoring in Pure and Applied Mathematics from Maseno University and currently pursuing masters in strategic management. She has over 4 years of practical experience as the head of operations overseeing the Finance, HR, Marketing, IT departments and other company operations. Anne holds the ever so important responsibility of ensuring the firm delivers only quality services and that its processes are efficient and effective.
