Job Summary.

    The Head of Operations is responsible for leading and managing multiple project teams to deliver exceptional results for our clients. This role is ultimately responsible for the general management of several projects and teams as well as the day to day operations.

    Operations

    Work hand-in-hand with the project team to create a mutual understanding of the project strategy based on the requirement of the job.
    Conduct in-depth reviews and analysis of all project or contract documents so as to be familiar with every detail and/or requirements.
    Assist project managers and engineers in the arrangement and analysis of projects and scheduling of reports on projects.
    Oversee projects as they are being carried out to make sure that the work is done according to outlined specifications, and that they also conform with project timetable.
    Ensure all the tools, equipment and materials are readily available for the workers and serialized.
    Ensuring budget costs relating to wages, contractors, materials, and equipment aren’t exceeded.
    Inspect and observe work in progress to ensure that procedures followed and materials used conformed to project specifications and quality standards.
    Establish and maintain effective Health and Safety system in place for the Projects strictly adhering to standards and specifications.

    Administrative

    Schedule and manage all requirements for regular internal meetings including: management meetings and Board meetings.
    Ensure agendas, papers and presentations are prepared and received for meetings and briefing Directors in advance where necessary.
    Take detailed minutes and accurately capture action points and follow up on actions.

    Support the Operations with administrative tasks relating to finance, governance, compliance and general operations Liaising with clients and suppliers assisting with placing order for materials needed.

    Procurement

    Liaise with relevant Departments to ascertain compliance and incorporate the data required in the Tender preparation.
    Coordinate and communicate proposal document delivery and costing requirements to meet proposal due dates while raising, tracking and documenting issues.
    Ensure all relevant documentation for bid proposals is complete and accurate Track the inventory on a daily basis and keep a record of the stock

    Human Resource

    Support daily human resources activities and responsibilities.
    Coordinate employee leave of absence.
    Manage employee records.
    Ensure efficient collaboration and coordination between departments through communication with managers for successful job completion

    Record keeping

    Verify all the company documents are completed accurately.
    Maintain employee records and compliance.
    Ensure compliance of all employees and subcontractors with local laws and company policy at all locations and job sites

    Education

    Bachelors/Diploma in project management.
    5+ years of construction site management experience.
    Expert knowledge of construction processes and quality standards.
    Proficient knowledge of quality standards and regulations.
    Background in Finance will be an added advantage
